IMDb Rating:

IMDb registered users can cast a vote (from 1 to 10) on
every released title in the database. Individual votes are then aggregated
and summarized as a single IMDb rating, visible on the title's main page.
(definition from IMDb official website)


Jump Scare Rating:

Contributors of the website, "Where's the Jump?", rate the jump scare
movies they collected and evaluate their intensity.
